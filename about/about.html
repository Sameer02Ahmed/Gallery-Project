About Us

Welcome to SRC Studio, where every shot tells a story, and each moment is captured with finesse. Founded with a passion for capturing lifes most memorable moments, our studio combines expert photography skills with a cinematic touch, inspired by our very own brand ambassador, Fawad Khan.
With years of experience in the field, our talented team specializes in creating high-quality portraits, wedding photography, commercial shoots, and artistic photo collections. Guided by the vision of Fawad Khan, known for Pakistani actor, producer, screenwriter, songwriter and singer known for his work in films and television. Khan is a recipient of several accolades, including a Filmfare Award, two Lux Style Awards, and six Hum Awards. Khan began his acting career on the television sitcom, Jutt and Bond, we aim to bring elegance and authenticity to every session.
Our studio is designed to make you feel comfortable and inspired, ensuring you shine through in every frame. We invite you to experience the difference that comes with a celebrity-led studio where art, professionalism, and vision come together. Lets make memories that last a lifetime.

I’m proud to be part of a team that not only creates beautiful photos but also unforgettable memories."
